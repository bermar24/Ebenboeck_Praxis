Workshop

Preventive training - Fit at the workplace

Aim of the workshop:

My preventive training offers companies and groups the opportunity to increase the health, well-being, and performance of their employees through targeted measures. In this workshop, participants learn how they can integrate health-promoting behaviors into their everyday lives, reduce stress, improve their productivity and quality of life in the long term, and get through their working day pain-free.

Who is the workshop suitable for?

The workshop is aimed at all companies and organizations that want to promote the well-being of their employees. Whether small teams, large companies, or departments - the workshop can be adapted to individual needs and is suitable for anyone who wants to create a healthy workplace. You can also book this workshop as a private group.

Contents of the workshop:

  • Ergonomics in the workplace: Correct posture, movement exercises, and tips for avoiding tension and pain.
  • Stress management: Techniques and exercises to reduce stress, improve sleep, and increase focus.
  • Healthy eating: Practical tips on eating the right food to boost energy and concentration.

Methods:

The workshop combines practice-oriented exercises with theoretical impulses. There will be room for individual concerns and questions so that each group can apply what they have learned directly to their own needs.

Duration and schedule:

The workshop is a full-day event (6-7h) with a lunch break. Snacks and drinks will be provided on-site, lunch will be organized by the participants themselves.
